**Course:** ECON 6018 (Master’s Level)  
**Instructor:** Teresa Ng  
**Date:** 6 February 2026  

This tutorial introduces **Differentiation** as a core mathematical tool for graduate-level economics, with a strong emphasis on **speed, accuracy, and intuition** for comparative statics and optimization problems. The session builds foundational differentiation skills required for microeconomic theory, econometrics, and dynamic optimization.

---

## Topics Covered

* Derivatives and limits  
* Basic differentiation rules  
* Sum, product, and quotient rules  
* Chain rule  
* Higher-order derivatives  
* Exponential and logarithmic functions
