This tutorial introduces **Matrix Algebra** concepts that are foundational for graduate-level econometrics and economic theory.  
The session emphasizes both intuition and technical accuracy, preparing students for matrix-based formulations used in linear regression and estimation theory.

---

## Topics Covered

* Inverse matrices  
* Key matrix properties  
* Determinants (order 2 and order 3)  
* Solving systems of equations using matrix methods
